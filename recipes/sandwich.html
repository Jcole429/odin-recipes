Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is an item of food consisting of two pieces of bread with meat, cheese, or other filling between them, eaten as a light meal.

Ingredients

  • Two pieces of bread
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Put one piece of bread down
  2. Place the meat on the first piece of bread
  3. Place the cheese on top of the meet
  4. Place the Lettuce on top of the cheese
  5. Place the tomato on top of the lettuce
  6. Place the other piece of bread on top of the tomato
